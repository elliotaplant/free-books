How to add free.books.sender@kindle.com to approved senders and find your Kindle email address

Back to the app

Log in to amazon.com and click the "Accounts & Lists" menu item

Navigate to "Your devices and content"

Select "Devices & Content" from the left side menu

Select "Manage digital content"

Enter the "Preferences" menu tab

Click the "Personal Document Settings" header

Open the "Add a new approved e-mail address" menu

Enter the address "free.books.sender@gmail.com" and then click "Add Address"

Finally, copy your "Send-to-Kindle E-Mail" for the device you'd like books sent to

Back to the app
